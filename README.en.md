# Atemschutzbuch

A digital respiratory protection logbook ("Atemschutzbuch") for small volunteer
fire departments in Germany.

## What is this?

German fire departments are legally required to keep a record for every
respiratory protection wearer (*Atemschutzgeräteträger*, AGT): deployments,
exercises, briefings, physical stress drills and medical fitness checks. In many
small departments this is still kept on paper or in unwieldy spreadsheets – error
prone, hard to evaluate, and a constant struggle to keep deadlines in view.

The **Atemschutzbuch** digitises this record: clear, traceable, and tailored to
the everyday reality of small departments.

- **Audience:** small volunteer fire departments.
- **Open source & self-hosted:** the code is open, the data stays with the department.
